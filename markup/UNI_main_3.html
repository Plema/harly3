Frequently asked questions

What is Unifinance?

Unifinance is a combination of swap and money market protocols.

What is Unilend?

Unilend is a decentralized non-custodial money market protocol where users can participate as loaner or borrowers.

Loaners provide liquidity to the market to earn a passive income, while borrowers are able to borrow from loaners.

What is Unidex?

Unidex is a protocol for multi-token automated market-making.

Unidex pools contain 2 or more tokens each with predetemined weights of the total pool value.

Is Unifinance decentralized?

Unifinance trying to be as decentralized as possible.

There is no team control. All protocol decisions going on proposal basis.

Is there a Unifinance Governance Token?

Yes, Unifinance Governance Token UNI, can be used to vote on proposals and guide the direction of the protocol
